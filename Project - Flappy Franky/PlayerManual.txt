========================
  Flappy Franky Manual
========================

Welcome to Flappy Franky! This manual provides detailed instructions on gameplay, controls, and additional information:

1. **Starting the Game:**
   - Double-click on the `flappy-game.exe` file to launch the game.

2. **Game Controls:**
   - Use the spacebar to make Franky flap and navigate between the pipes.
   - Press 'M' to mute the background music.
   - Press 'U' to unmute the background music.

3. **Objective:**
   - Guide Franky through the pipes by tapping the spacebar or clicking to avoid collisions.

4. **Scoring System:**
   - Score points for every pipe passed. Aim for a high score by navigating through as many pipes as possible.

5. **Game Over:**
   - The game ends when Franky collides with a pipe. Try to beat your high score in subsequent attempts.

6. **Pause/Resume Feature:**
   - Press the 'P' key to pause the game. Press 'Enter' again to resume from where you left off.

7. **Troubleshooting:**
   - If the game encounters issues, check the README file for troubleshooting steps.

Enjoy playing Flappy Franky and have a fantastic gaming experience!
